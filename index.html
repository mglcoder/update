Welcome to Dhan Bhagya Nidhi Ltd

Dhan Bhagya Nidhi Ltd Prayagraj is a Public company which is duly approved by the Government of India, Ministry of Corporate Affairs as Nidhi Company under the companies Act, 2013 and that the company is limited by the share.
As Nidhi We accept deposits from our members/shareholders within the stipulated norms issued by the Ministry of Corporate Affairs. The funds mobilized are utilized to a great extent for advancing of Gold Loans, Property Loans, & Business Loans. Thus, the funds are fully secured and therefore the investments with us are in very safer havens. We believe that the success of business comes from each individual's creativity and team work. We at Dhan Bhagya Nidhi Limited, stimulate the mutual growth of our members through Trust and Responsibility and in this process Dhan Bhagya Nidhi Limited strives to contribute to the development of the society overall.
