A Nidhi shall give loans to its members only against the following securities, namely:

1. Fixed deposit receipts, National Savings Certificates, other Government Securities and insurance policies: Provided that such securities duly discharged shall be pledged with Nidhi and the maturity date of such securities shall not fall beyond the loan period or one year whichever is earlier. 2. Provided further that in the case of loan against fixed deposits, the period of loan shall not exceed the unexpired period of the fixed deposits.

Terms and Conditions:

  • Repayment period of such loan available before the date of maturity of Deposit.
  • Repayment is available earlier as you can repay as you desire.
  • Minor are not eligible for loan.
  • Loan amount will be sanctioned 70% of accrued value of Deposit./li>
  • Simple interest will be charges.
  • The interest rate is fixed and calculated on a reducing balance basis.
  • In case of default notice charges and other charges will be applicable.
  • In case of delay penalty will be charges.
